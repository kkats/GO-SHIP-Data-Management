LADCP

Archives

  • NCEI

    • Follow https://www.ncei.noaa.gov → Access Data → Launch NOAA Onestop → search “LADCP”.
    • To download data, try Granule Search → Download (HTTP)
    • Processed and raw data (including CTD and GPS timeseries) are available!

  • US GO-SHIP Hydrotable

  • JAMSTEC (currently unavailale)

    • processed ASCII output only. MATLAB files may be available upon request to k.katsumata@jamstec.go.jp

  • BODC

    • processed ASCII or NetCDF. Select Platform as lowered unmanned submersible and find cruise

  • BODC-request

    • Data do not appear in BODC currents database, but are listed as being held by BODC; individual profiles may be accessible by searching for cruise name or contact enquiries@bodc.ac.uk

  • NOC

    • Contact yvonne.firing@noc.ac.uk about processed data.
